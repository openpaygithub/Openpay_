OPENPAY VOUCHER SPECIAL OFFER TERMS & CONDITIONS

 

To be Eligible:

  • To be eligible to receive a Voucher Customers must make a purchase at a participating Merchant (Issuing Merchant) to the value of $100 or more and pay using Openpay between 1 March 2019 and 1 January 2020 unless extended.

  • Car purchases, alloy wheels and warranty products are excluded.

  • Only available at Issuing Merchants. See a list of participating Issuing Merchants below.

  • One (1) Voucher available per plan.

  • One (1) person cannot take out multiple plans on the same date.


To Redeem:

  • To redeem the Voucher Customers must make a purchase at a participating Merchant (Redeeming Merchant) to a minimum value of $150 (unless otherwise indicated in the issuing email and point of sale) and pay using Openpay before thirty (30) days has elapsed. See a list of participating Redeeming Merchants below.

  • Vouchers can be redeemed in-store by presenting the Voucher code at a Redeeming Merchants store or online through the Redeeming Merchants website by inputting the Voucher code at checkout.

  • Vouchers expire thirty (30) days after being issued to the Customer.

  • Provided the redeeming conditions are satisfied, the Customer will receive a voucher valued at $25 or $50 (Depending on redeeming merchants). Customers must use the entire voucher amount in the one purchase.


Other:

  • Vouchers can only be redeemed by the intended recipient. Openpay will not be responsible if a Voucher code is lost or stolen. In the event of a lost or stolen Voucher code the Customer will not be eligible to receive a new Voucher.

  • Refunds will only be provided to the Customer in accordance with the Redeeming Merchants refund policy. In the event that a Customer refunds an Openpay plan to which the Voucher was originally applied, the Customer will only be refunded the amount actually paid to Openpay at the time of the refund. Customers will not be refunded the Voucher amount.

  • The Voucher amount will be applied to the customers future payments to reduce the overall dollar ($) amount of each instalment. The amount will not be deducted from the purchase price of the customers items and the initial deposit amount to be paid at checkout will be the advised % of the full purchase price.

  • Openpay reserves the right to change, extend or withdraw the offer at any time. This includes varying the purchase eligibility, purchase redemption and voucher values. Issuing and Redeeming Merchants are subject to change.
