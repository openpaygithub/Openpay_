OPENPAY PROMOTION CODE TERMS & CONDITIONS

 

  1. From time to time promotion codes will be made available to customers of Openpay.

  2. Promotion codes are subject to the terms and conditions detailed on the Openpay website and will be valid for the period of time specified.

  3. Promotion codes can only be redeemed by the intended recipient. Openpay will not be responsible if a promotion code is lost or stolen. In the event of a lost or stolen promotion code, the customer will not be eligible to receive a new promotion code.

  4. The promotion code amount will be applied to the customer’s future payments to reduce the overall dollar ($) amount of each instalment. The promotion code amount will not be deducted from the purchase price of the customer’s items and the initial deposit amount to be paid at checkout will be the advised percentage of the full purchase price.

  5. Refunds will only be provided to a customer in accordance with applicable law and, if also applicable, the Merchant’s refund policy. In the event that a customer receives a refund in respect of an Openpay plan to which the promotion code was originally applied, the customer will only be refunded the amount actually paid by the customer to Openpay as at the time of the refund. Customers will not be refunded the promotion code or the promotion code amount.

  6. Openpay reserves the right to change, extend or withdraw an offer at any time. This includes varying the purchase eligibility criteria, purchase redemption and promotion code values. Merchants are subject to change.

  7. Promotion codes cannot be used in conjunction with any other Openpay promotion or offers.
