Smile now, pay smarter.

Whether it's a check-up, clean or major dental treatment, you can now get the treatment you need today and may be able to pay overtime with an Openpay Payment Plan at eligible Bupa Dental clinics.

How does Openpay work?

1. Book your visit

Choose to transact with Openpay

2. Flexible plans

The plan is processed in-clinic via the Openpay App or web portal. You pay the first instalment

3. Enjoy your smile

Pay off the remaining instalments over 2-24 months

*Approved applicants only. Establishment and plan management fees may apply depending on your plan amount. See the table below.
